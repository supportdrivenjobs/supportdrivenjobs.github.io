ipsy Customer Care Content Manager

ipsy is committed to providing excellent service to our active community. We’re seeking a Customer Care Content Manager who create and optimize top-notch content (both text and visual) for our customers and employees. You will focus on scaling our online help center to maximize customer engagement and education, enhance accessibility, and reduce inbound volume through effective self-help material. You will also be responsible for creating and maintaining standard response templates, write responses for large-scale and sensitive issues in collaboration with other managers and stakeholders, and maintain our support team’s internal knowledge base.

Content Manager Profile:

  • Innovative and Analytical: You are a wordsmith, data-driven, and passionate about developing creative solutions.
  • Organized and Articulate: You are comfortable owning an array of detailed responsibilities. You communicate effectively across multiple mediums (written, oral, visual).
  • Proactive and Adaptable: You thrive in a challenging, collaborative environment and respond to changes with a flexible attitude.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Write and edit community help center (FAQs), customer outreach emails, and response templates for our support agents
  • Maintain content for our internal knowledge base, as well as style and tone resources for our agents
  • Optimize help center content using data analysis in order to improve the customer experience and reduce contacts
  • Identify opportunities for context-sensitive content across customer service channels, anticipate where customers may need support, and define information delivery strategies

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in content management, preferably in a customer service capacity
  • Experience defining, tracking, and reporting metrics on the effectiveness of content
  • Basic understanding of Google Analytics
  • Excellent eye for detail
  • Understanding of and appreciation for best-in-class customer experience
  • Empathetic, authentic, and straightforward writing style
  • Strong leadership skills and ability to take initiative
  • Capable of managing a large volume and variety of work
  • Knowledge of HTML and/or CSS a plus
  • Basic graphic design and/or Adobe Photoshop skills a plus

About us:

ipsy is the world’s largest and most passionate beauty community. The community is centered around a beauty sampling service called the Glam Bag and premier content produced by ipsy and its partner creators. Since being founded in 2011 by top YouTube creator Michelle Phan, the company has grown dramatically. On the sampling front, ipsy ships to over 1.5 million subscribers every month; on the owned content side, ipsy reaches over 20 million people every month through stylist videos and social posts.

ipsy has built a results-oriented team packed with seasoned beauty, e-commerce, tech, and studio professionals in offices in the Bay Area, NYC, and LA. The company has received over $100M in growth capital from premier investors including TPG, Sherpa and top industry insiders.
