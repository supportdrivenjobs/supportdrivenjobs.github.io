UltraLinq Healthcare Solutions Implementation Specialist

Implementation Specialists are responsible for the on-boarding process for prospects and new clients. Implementation Specialists must work with the on-boarding client to assess product needs, manage technical operations and roadblocks, and appropriately train the user base. Implementation Specialists must also participate in customer support and troubleshooting when necessary.

Job Responsibilities

  • Manage the on-boarding process for new clients.
  • Accurate logging, dispatching and resolution of 1st and 2nd level support requests.
  • Tracking of client feature requests.
  • User training for all client personnel who require access to the product.
  • After-hours support as required.

Job Skills

  • Familiarity with the project management lifecycle.
  • Ability to work independently with strong time management skills.
  • Excellent written communication skills; including above average grammar and spelling ability.
  • Understanding of the principles and process of troubleshooting.
  • Ability to communicate clearly, calmly and in an articulate manner over the phone.
  • Advanced competency regarding Microsoft Windows, web browsers, and a special focus on network and firewall configuration.
  • Strong understanding of networking principles (TCP, DNS, DHCP, etc.) and networking hardware setup.
  • Advanced knowledge of the UltraLinq and UpLinq applications.
  • Knowledge of medical workflows or familiarity with medical imaging devices, strongly preferred.
  • A pleasant demeanor and patient attitude.

Other Stuff:

  • Ideal candidate is local to London
  • Implementation Specialists are required to sit for extended periods of time.
  • Client Services Representatives must utilize a computer with a mouse for extended periods of time.
  • Travel: Approximately 25%
