Wildbit Customer Success - Postmarkapp

Got questions about Wildbit?

We’re looking for someone who understands that customer success is a focus on customers, not on ourselves. At Wildbit, our focus is entirely on enabling teams to build and launch their own products and services. Our success team handles our customers needs proactively (success) and reactively (support) across our three prodcuts (Beanstalk, DeployBot, and Postmark).

You’ll be working with our customer success team helping new customers get up to speed quickly, educating developers on industry best practices, and helping our team identify where we can better meet the needs of our customers. This means creating content & user guides, talking to customers over various channels (email, support, chat, phone, etc), analyzing behaviors, and keeping our developers & designers up to speed at all times.

Most of all, this job is about empowering developers to do their best work. And to have fun doing it!

Requirements:

  • solid writing skills
  • empathy for novice developers
  • a desire to teach
  • a solid understanding of transactional email and email technologies (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • experience in measuring and tracking your own results
  • for this position, we’re looking someone able to work the following hours: 8–4 PST

Nice to haves:

  • previous experience in customer success/product management
  • previous experience in customer support
  • previous experience supporting or building a Saas product

Benefits:

  • Chef-prepared family lunches (in Philly office)
  • Full health benefits
  • Company-paid retreats
  • Computer of your choice, renewed every 2 years
  • 25 paid days off per year
  • Quarterly profit sharing
  • Attend conferences
  • Quiet Fridays
  • 40 hour work week with flexible hours
