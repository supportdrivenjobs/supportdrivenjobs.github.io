Formlabs Professional Support Lead

Got questions about Formlabs?

To reinvent an industry, you have to build the best team. Join Formlabs if you want to bring ground-breaking professional 3D printers to the desktop of every designer, engineer, researcher, and artist in the world.

Our primary goal is to give our customers the best tools with an amazing experience. We’re looking for a visionary that can lead a team to ensure the highest level of service for our growing professional user base: the individual users and the larger organizations that have opted for our Pro Service Plan.

If you love building relationships and redefining the customer experience, we want you on the Form Team as the Professional Support Lead.

The job:

  • Develop new channels and resources for a rapidly growing customer base
  • Create excellent support content and continually improve our support systems
  • Educate customers on new ways to get more from their printers
  • Keep customers ridiculously happy with a true white glove experience
  • Print in 3D to your heart’s content

You:

  • Have an excellent track record of building and managing a product or service
  • Champion technical solutions alongside engineers, designers, and fabricators
  • A visionary, with a love for getting on the phone to consult customers on their needs
  • Have developed curriculum and/or taught complex technical issues
  • Enjoy dismantling, troubleshooting, and playing with electronics
  • Care about going the extra mile to meet each customer’s needs
  • Possess exceptional written and verbal communication skills
  • Have worked on the front lines of customer service
  • Easily adopt cloud-based business systems (Salesforce, Zendesk)
  • Can build and excite a team to consistently deliver high quality service

Bonus skills:

  • Experience with 3D printing, modeling and digital fabrication tools
  • Experience providing technical support for hardware products
  • Multi-lingual: fluency in German or French
