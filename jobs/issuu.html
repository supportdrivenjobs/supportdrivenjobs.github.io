About this job

The marketing team at issuu is seeking a creative, inspired Community Manager to join us and take responsibility for building strong, positive relationships with the growing number of publishers who rely on issuu every day. You enjoy interacting with people online and in the real world, and you have a knack for engaging others with the ideas and content you create and share. You’ll be part of a growing team that collaborates with awesome colleagues in design, support, product and engineering to deliver the world’s best publishing experience for millions of people. This is a full-time position based in issuu’s Palo Alto office. You will report to the VP of Marketing.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Manage issuu social communities across Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Instagram and others
  • Engage with publishers to foster the development of an active community organized around their concerns, interests and opportunities for growth
  • Identify and manage influencer outreach to drive additional visibility for issuu among publishers
  • Lead the creation and management of content and social media campaigns to highlight issuu’s network and global audience
  • Develop an editorial calendar and content strategy for each social channel we participate in

QUALIFICATIONS

  • 1-3 years of experience managing online communities
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Strong understanding and prior experience with major social media platforms including Facebook, Twitter and Pinterest
  • Great teammate and collaborator, able to work with a diverse and geographically dispersed team
  • Able to be effective in a startup environment: be proactive, creative and innovative

BENEFITS

  • Competitive compensation package, commensurate with experience
  • Flexible vacation policy
  • Competitive health, dental and vision packages
  • A sleek Mac and a pretty sweet desk setup
  • Walking distance to the Palo Alto Caltrain station and monthly passes for public transportation or parking
  • Catered lunches and team events
