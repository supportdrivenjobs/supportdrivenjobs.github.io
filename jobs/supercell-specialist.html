Supercell Player Support Outsourcing Specialist (Japan)

Japan is a country with a long and rich tradition in the video game industry, home to some of the most beloved brands in the world – and to some of the most passionate players of our games.

We are looking for a Player Support Outsourcing Specialist to join our team in Helsinki and to work with our partners in Japan to ensure the best support experience for our players. Working in our multicultural team you will be the voice of our players in Japan, manage the relationship with our local partner and build a bridge between the eastern and western cultures.

The person we are looking for is a gamer with a strong background in customer support or vendor management and a unicorn-like personality who wants to push the boundaries of support for mobile games and beyond.

You are self-driven and hands-on? You can take decisions on a day-to-day basis independently? You don’t mind getting your hands dirty and address our players on individual level? Great, we have a team structure with minimal hierarchy and no red tape where the only limits to what’s achievable are set by your imagination.

If you want to make a difference for millions of fellow players in Japan, this might be the right place for you.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Become an expert at Supercell games and our internal tools
  • Directly manage our BPO partner in Japan
  • Use data in your day-to-day management to achieve the best results
  • Collaborate closely with other internal stakeholders
  • Be the voice of our players in Japan
  • Apply your knowledge to current and new Player Support projects
  • Support the Japanese localization of our games as required

REQUIREMENTS

  • Native or bi-lingual Japanese skills
  • Excellent verbal and written English skills
  • Previous experience in a customer support or vendor management (3-5 years) role
  • Team player with strong problem solving and conflict management skills
  • Empathic and culturally aware
  • Passion for video games and mobile applications
  • Ability to travel frequently (other Supercell offices and partners)

NICE TO HAVES

  • Passion for one or multiple Supercell games
  • Experience working in multicultural environments

SUPERCELL OFFERS

  • A first-row view into one of the most successful game companies in the world
  • Opportunity to work with a team of gaming veterans, top talent, and passionate gamers
  • Easy-going, enthusiastic, open and at the same time a very results-driven culture
  • Competitive compensation
  • Smartphone, tablet, laptop/workstation of your own choice
  • Industry leading benefits
  • Amazing co-workers
