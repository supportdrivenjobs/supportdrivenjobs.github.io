Klaviyo Support Engineer

Email will no longer suck. Klaviyo is using technology to reinvent email marketing from the ground up! For far too long email marketing has been pretty selfish. Companies blast everyone with the same email, not because they think that’s what customers want, but because they don’t have a choice.

Klaviyo fundamentally changes the game. Our software ties together everything companies know about their customers, processes massive quantities of data in real-time, and uses it to drive a platform that’s part CRM, part analytics engine and 100% an email platform that drives results.

Technology has come a long way in the last 15 years, but email hasn’t really changed. We’re here to fix that.

Success at Klaviyo

  • Serve as the Klaviyo Product Expert

You Have

  • Proficiency with APIs
