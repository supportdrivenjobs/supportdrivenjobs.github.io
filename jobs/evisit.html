eVisit Customer Success Coach

Across the SaaS (Software as a Service) industry, the role of Customer Success is being developed; yet because it’s so new, people usually do not understand what it is. In essence, it’s all about customer service, support and satisfaction. Gone are the days of a simple sale. A new era has begun where a company must nurture its customers to drive renewals and up-sell revenue through a long-term, engineered strategy; thus, the development of “Customer Success.”

As a Customer Success Coach, this team member will support the Director of Customer Success in the active management of the eVisit customer experience, engagement and satisfaction.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Manage all aspects of day-to-day activities, such as provisioning customer accounts, collecting and analyzing customer information, preparing product or service reports, maintaining financial accounts, and processing renewals/upsells.
  • Administer customer product training to ensure strong adoption, usage and ongoing engagement.
  • Play a leading role in empowering and advising customers on optimal approaches to implementation and patient adoption challenges based on intimate understanding of product, industry and customer needs.
  • Support in the endeavor to provide exemplary customer service, using empathy and logical reason to answer questions and troubleshoot problems, documenting such interactions in detail and ensuring prompt resolution.
  • Oversee periodic customer success reviews to help ensure operational effectiveness and customer satisfaction and drive continuous improvement of platform through careful consideration of customer feedback.
  • Develop materials for customers to assist in driving eVisit’s customers’ best possible product use and satisfaction, including tutorial videos, job aids, knowledge-based articles and new feature/product releases emails.
  • Support in efforts to increase renewal rates, reduce churn and expand revenue through cross-sell and up-sell opportunities.
  • Perform ad-hoc projects and research as needed.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Undergraduate Bachelor degree a plus.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience in customer service in the medical/healthcare industry.
  • Minimum of 4-6 years in customer service with a SaaS organization a plus. (the medical/healthcare industry desired).
  • Familiarity with medical insurance, billing and HIPAA compliance a plus.
  • Experience with medical and/or healthcare marketing a plus.
  • Ability to be flexible and adapt quickly in a fast-paced, start-up environment.
  • Excellent writing and verbal communication skills; with a superb attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Self-motivated with keen organizational skills, time-management, and multi-tasking skills.
  • Personable, reliable relationship-builder who cares about the well-being, success and happiness of customers.
  • Works well under pressure and tight deadlines within a fast-paced startup.
  • Natural ability to lead, think creatively and find solutions.

BENEFITS AND PERKS:

  • Competitive Salary based on experience.
  • Premium Health Insurance Reimbursement program, where eVisit offers its Full Time Employees a monthly healthcare allowance to purchase their own selected health insurance.
  • Unlimited Personal Time Off Policy. The first 15 days of PTO is paid time off, any PTO taken after the first 15 days is unlimited, but unpaid. In addition to the traditional 8 paid national holidays.
  • Fun, collaborative environment where the company is working to define the future of telemedicine.
  • Hands-on experience in a startup environment.
  • Work with a team of experienced entrepreneurs with proven track record of launching, building and selling startups.
  • Significant opportunity for professional growth and promotion.
