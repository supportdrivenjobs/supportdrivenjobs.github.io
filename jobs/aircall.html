Aircall Technical Support Hero

You'll join the US business team to ensure that the customers we work with have a great experience. You will be the first point of contact and advocate for clients.

What are the responsibilities:

  • Be the voice of Aircall - you will be on the front lines! This involves flexing your amazing troubleshooting skills via phone calls, emails, and live chat.
  • Provide swift and thorough solutions for customers. When absolutely unable to do so, escalate to the dev/tech team.
  • Take note and relay customer feedback and insights to Aircall's internal teams such as the tech, marketing and product teams, in order to constantly improve the customer experience.
  • Maintain an expert-level knowledge of our products and internal management systems as well as curiosity and awareness of new initiatives within in the industry.

What we are looking for:

  • You have at least 1+ year of experience in Support and are passionate about it.
  • You are technical and problem solver. UDP/TCP ports, REST API and packet losses do mean something to you.
  • You have a “no task is too small” attitude and the endurance to consistently deliver amazing customer experiences without exception.
  • You have superior written and oral communication skills.
  • You have strong people skills — you are friendly, a good listener, you ask good questions, and you're energized by personal interaction.
  • You are a team player who works well with a wide range of personalities and is ready to contribute to an awesome team environment.

Brownie points:

  • You have a sense of humour and know how to make fun of yourself.
  • Flexibility with schedule to meet the needs of the business.

What are the benefits of working here?

  • All the caffeine you need to keep you going
  • Medical/Vision/Dental benefits
  • Tax-free commuter benefits
  • Your metrocard reimbursed
  • Gym reimbursement
  • Shiny new Mac gear!
