SmugMug Support Process Manager

Got questions about SmugMug?
@benmacaskill can answer them in the Support Driven Chat

The Support Process Manager would be responsible for driving continual improvement and excellence in every customer interaction at SmugMug. They would need to bring an academic and data driven approach to making sure we are thrilling our customers at every possible chance. They would be responsible for owning support processes, language and tone, team training and education, tooling, and product insights and improvements.

The ideal candidate should be comfortable doing self-driven research, in large scale data and in small scale interactions. The results of that research needs to be insight, but also actionable. They need to be comfortable working closely with all teams at SmugMug, especially Product and Design teams. This role should be one of the best resources to those teams to understand our customers and how they interact with our product and our support team.

Your Role

  • Own the entire data collection and reporting for Support
  • Proactively improve support policies and procedures, driving improvements across the entire team
  • Ongoing education and training for the Support Team
  • Driving engagement with Support for the entire company

Must Have

  • Proven track record leading successful Support teams and policies
  • Experience managing ticketing systems such as Zendesk or Desk.com
  • Experience with research and data driven decision making
  • Passion for continued learning

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Project Management
  • Proven skill with data collection and analysis tools
  • Passion for photography
