ABOUT US:

Envoy's mission is to have awesome visitor and employee experiences become universal in the workplace. With our first product – Envoy Visitor Registration – we’re reimagining the future of the front desk.

Envoy is an all-in-one visitor management solution. From collecting visitor information and signatures to host notification, visitor pre-registration, and more. Thousands of companies rely on Envoy to elegantly welcome visitors and keep their information safe and secure. Our focus on good design and customer service set us apart from the myriad other enterprise software companies that settle for 'good enough.'

Envoy is backed by Andreessen Horowitz and has served over 4M visitors in 55 countries since it was founded in 2013. Envoy is used by some of today’s most exciting brands including Box, Pinterest, Pixar, GoPro and Mailchimp.

ABOUT YOU:

You’re self-motivated, love making people happy, and have a talent for resolving customer issues with a smile. As an Envoy Customer Ambassador, you’ll be responsible for creating amazing customer experiences, helping us improve our team workflow, and putting out the occasional fire.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Qualify, prioritize, and close customer issues.
  • Monitor and address customer tickets and Live Chats.
  • Work with the team to identify, report, and resolve issues and file bug reports.
  • Work with customers to trouble-shoot technical problems, ranging from integrations to hardware.
  • Understand common customer issues to better inform support processes.
  • Champion the customer's issues and feature requests with the product team.
  • Help refine the processes for the Envoy Ambassadors team.

MUST HAVES:

  • Experience working with customers, either in a call center or retail position.
  • Desire to go above and beyond to help customers achieve the best experience.
  • Passion for problem solving and the right attitude and aptitude for learning new technology.
  • Excellent written and verbal skills; ability to explain the complex in a simple manner.
  • Love talking to people, and desire to help people through tough issues.
