I Will Teach You to be Rich Customer Success Representative

About Us

At I Will Teach You to Be Rich, we’re passionate about helping millions of people live richer lives, from starting businesses to finding their dream jobs to even losing weight and becoming more fit.

Our programs attract thousands of customers worldwide and we strive to deliver top-notch customer support every step of the way.

The Opportunity

We are looking for an experienced Customer Success Representative who can help us develop our customer support operations to the highest level inside of our dynamic growth phase, as well as create the structures and stability necessary to sustain this growth into the future. Someone who is passionate about customer service and how it can transform businesses.

As Customer Success Representative, you’ll be accountable for addressing hundreds of customer interactions daily across multiple platforms, including email, live chat and phone. On the front lines of communication with our students, you’ll be instrumental in capturing feedback from our students and channeling it to other teams across the organization to help us deliver the highest quality products and services.

You’ll be trained in all our programs and master the frameworks, tools, and techniques we’ve created to change people’s behavior and help them achieve their aspirations. You'll be immersed in the technologies, systems and processes we use to power our business. This is an opportunity to get in on the ground floor of our rapidly growing company and have a direct impact with our students. Our customer service efforts are evolving and we need people who can evolve with us.

How You’ll Contribute:

Be an IWT Ambassador

  • Provide superior customer service for customers by demonstrating an in-depth knowledge of the company’s programs, procedures, and policies.
  • Master the company’s course offerings, effectively identify and recommend best courses for student’s needs.
  • Respond rapidly and thoroughly to customer inquiries. We strive for an immediate response time during business hours and use an online interface that tracks all communications.

Product Launches

  • Be an essential part of the success of our monthly product launches. This includes:
  • Provide assistance in preparation of course launches, including proofreading, editing, and testing of products, transactions and community sites.
  • Provide pre-sales support to prospective customers in support of course launches, and advise customers on courses that best fit their needs.
  • Monitor webinars, live chat, email, and phone.

Process Improvement

  • This is a unique opportunity to not only represent IWT on the front-lines, but to also dive in and give insight on how we can improve our processes.
  • Use your creative juices and deep knowledge of our customer base to develop tools such as FAQs, screencast tutorials, help pages, etc, to continually improve the user support experience.
  • Assist with evaluating, implementing and maintaining customer service technology and systems, including workflow, help desk, phone systems, chat systems, etc.
  • Use clear judgment to identify and promptly communicate relevant trends to the Product Development team and technical issues to the IT team.
  • Assist in creating and maintaining training protocols, macros and other processes to keep customer success team continually up-to-date on the company’s products and services.

About You and What You Bring

  • Experience in providing best-in-class communication in a fast-paced, high volume, customer support environment
  • 2-5 years experience in online customer support role, responding to a high volume of customer inquiries.
  • You communication style (verbal and written) is clear, concise and reflective of the highest standards of professionalism and customer care.
  • You are exceptionally detail oriented. You believe that it is of utmost important that there are no spelling errors/typos in your communication with our customers.
  • You have surpassed KPI’s in terms of productivity and performance in previous roles (volume of tickets per week, tickets per hour, and customer satisfaction).

Tech-savvy

  • You are technically inclined and can pick up new systems and technologies quickly.
  • You have broad knowledge of browsers, MAC and PC platforms, mobile devices, WordPress, and collaboration tools.
  • You have working knowledge of Google Apps, Excel, MS Office products and other leading productivity applications.

Initiative and Flexibility

  • You are a self-starter / self-motivated, Able to work independently with minimal oversight.
  • You are flexible and can adapt your work schedule to align with peak periods and demands of our business.

Skills you’ll need in order to succeed in this role:

  • Bachelor’s Degree.
  • 2-5 years experience in online customer service or comparable experience.
  • Prior working knowledge of Zendesk or other leading help desk platform.
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills with the ability to forge meaningful connections with students using email and chat.
  • Outstanding attitude - readily accepts change, takes initiative, and responds positively to suggestions and constructive criticism.
  • Experience or ability to work remotely (with your own phone, headset, laptop and high speed internet access in place).

Bonus points for:

  • Familiarity with IWT products and services and a subscriber to our blog.
  • Working knowledge of InfusionSoft or other popular CRM systems.
  • Analytics and reporting experience in a customer support function.
  • Pacific time zone coverage and/or weekend availability.

This is 100% virtual job - you can work from anywhere (Must be US-based).
