Fleetio Customer Experience Rep

Got questions about Fleetio?
@corey can answer them in the Support Driven Chat

As a Customer Experience Rep for Fleetio, your job is to support our customers and enable their success. This is a demanding but rewarding role that requires a broad skillset. You’ll be directly responsible for keeping Fleetio’s customers happy and productive. You will also work with the product teams to test new features and provide feedback based on customer conversations.

Companies around the world rely on Fleetio to manage their fleet operations. Your job is to ensure that Fleetio’s customers have the best customer service experience possible.

We take customer service seriously. This is a position we consider essential to the company’s success. Be sure to mention coffee in your cover letter so we know you actually read this.

Responsibilities

  • Handle inbound customer requests via email and phone (90% email, 10% phone)
  • Quickly resolve customer inquiries with helpful, personalized responses
  • Identify, reproduce and document bugs for the product teams
  • Assist the product teams by testing new features and bug fixes
  • Spot trends in customer issues and communicate to product teams
  • Write documentation and contribute content to our Help Center
  • Create screencasts (video tutorials) for specific features and functionality
  • Monitor and report on support metrics and KPIs and suggest improvements

REQUIREMENTS

  • Interest in working Eastern Timezone hours, Monday through Friday
  • Genuine empathy for customers, and desire to make them feel happy and understood
  • Passion for exceptional customer service and appreciation for its role in a company’s success
  • Excellent writing and communication skills
  • Resourceful and determined, yet not afraid to ask for help
  • Natural troubleshooting skills
  • Serious attention to detail
  • Excited by technology and its ability to make you more productive
  • Prior experience doing email-centric customer support for a web application is highly desirable
  • College degree or equivalent professional experience preferred

BENEFITS

  • 100% coverage of health and dental insurance 401(k)
  • Competitive salary and meaningful equity
  • Open PTO policy
  • Mac laptop and whatever tools you need
