EventMobi Support Specialist

EventMobi is changing the way event planners create, market and manage their events, as well as how attendees experience events using their smartphones and tablets for thousands of events, conferences and tradeshows around the world. Clients like Motorola, Ralph Lauren, Nestle, Intel and Canadian Heart & Stroke Foundation have used EventMobi to enhance the attendee experience at their events.

Want to quickly figure out what EventMobi is all about? Then watch this fun 3min video here: http://www.eventmobi.com/careers/#video

Wondering how our technology is affecting the world and the meeting industry in general? Well here is an awesome infographic that summarizes what we have achieved this past and why we are super excited for 2016: http://www.eventmobi.com/about/company/2015/

What impact will you have?

  • First point of contact for our amazing clients when they need help - Our clients are generally professional event planners working at associations or large corporations. -The end user of our product however are attendees around the world from all ages and professions.
  • Lead client onboarding process from end-to-end
  • Handle inquiries about app development and troubleshoot client needs
  • Take accountability to solve issues from beginning to end.
  • Liaise with Engineering team to help drive feature development for our product
  • Receive and incorporate client feedback to improve business performance
  • Proactively profile customer needs and match our products and services to those needs
  • This is a chance to learn and be involved at every point of the company while helping and building relationships with a wide range of EventMobi clients across the world.
  • 10% travel is required: our clients often need our support team to help in the execution of their event
  • Limited evening and weekend work may be required to help with peak times based on client needs

What will you bring to the team?

  • An empathetic desire to help: For you, a new inquiry isn’t a just case to close, but an opportunity to understand someone’s story and make their life better. Our purpose as a company is to help event planners thrive.
  • Superb communication skills: You understand that effective communication is about more than what you say, it’s about taking the time to listen and understand. You can make complicated concepts sound easy or describe in detail the simplest of interactions.
  • A deep understanding of software and the web: You won’t need to know how to code, but terms like HTML and CSS shouldn’t be foreign to you. You can quickly learn complex procedures, troubleshoot potential bugs on the fly (pun intended), and visualize customers’ screens from their description alone.
  • Prior customer facing experience: Whether it was building a rapport with customers at Starbucks/Apple, or working in a customer-centric call centre, you must have experience working with customers.
  • Strong interpersonal skills - ability to work with people with a variety of skill sets
  • Can you make good choices that serve both the customer and company? We need folks who are passionate about delighting and retaining customers.
