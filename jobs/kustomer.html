Kustomer Customer Experience Associate

As a Customer Experience Associate, helping people is your passion. Improving the state of customer experience is our mission. Our goal is to build trusted advisor relationships which results in happy, loyal customers and colleagues. We do this by listening to people’s needs and guiding them through the best use of the products we offer. Today, we are looking for people with the right mix of compassion, communication skills, and technical knowledge to make customer experience the best it can be.

You’ll be responsible for:

  • Helping people understand how to use Kustomer’s platform and products
  • Onboarding new customers and partnering with them to answer their questions
  • Troubleshooting, investigating, and creating detailed bug reports for customer issues
  • Crafting and editing helpful support documentation and FAQs
  • Building a community of support by sharing knowledge and insight amongst team members
  • Being an active member of the team that provides support to our users via live chat, phone and email

Your qualities:

  • A technical understanding of computer science (degree or bootcamp) is required
  • Great communication skills; frequent and clear written and verbal is vital for success
  • A knack for taking technical language and making it understandable
  • Self-driven work ethic: be a self-starter who takes initiative and sees projects through to completion.
  • Customer service experience to help our customers improve their processes
  • Curiosity and the desire to learn. Our business is changing and growing fast, flexibility is key
  • Empathy, patience, grace, and a sense of humor
  • Experience solving tough problems and proposing elegant solutions
  • Fluent in English and bonus points if you know additional languages
