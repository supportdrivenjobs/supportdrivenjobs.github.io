WHAT WE DO:

Sched helps event organizers publish event info and track attendees. We make it easy to create a beautiful event site with a schedule and directory of your speakers, sponsors and attendees. Thousands of conferences, conventions, festivals, and meetings depend on Sched including Comic-Con, Wanderlust, Stanford, Austin Film Festival and even the US Grand Prix.

WHAT YOU'RE LIKE:

  • Love to help others & empathize with users of all experience levels
  • Have outstanding writing & communication skills
  • Enjoy solving problems & learning new things
  • Reliable, optimistic & super-organized
  • Tech-savvy
  • Experience doing online support

WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING:

As part of the Sched support team, you will be a critical part of creating great events & providing support for customers. Your primary responsibilities include email & live-chat support for our customers, and improving our support articles. You will also work side-by-side with our development team to identify areas of improvement and help determine new Sched features based on customer insights.

Culture
