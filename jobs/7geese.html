7Geese Customer Support Manager

Got questions about 7Geese?

7Geese is seeking an energetic and talented Customer Support Manager with a passion for helping customers and a relentless desire to improve customer support best practices.

In this role, you will own customer support interactions, resources, processes, and strategy. You will work very closely with the Customer Success and Product Development teams to collaborate on support resources and engagement projects.

At 7Geese, customer support means delighting customers with positive and memorable experiences that provide outcome-based solutions. We believe that finding and seeking help shouldn't be a chore. Do you agree?

Start Date: ASAP

Our guide to hiring rockstar support teammates:

You invite scrutiny and exude positivity. Not every interaction is going to involve a ‘yes’, but you believe you can still delight and offer help. Honesty and empathy are your biggest soft-skills.

You avoid negativity and have integrity. You believe feedback of all kinds can lead to improvement. There are insightful opportunities in every interaction.

You are proactive and love teaching others. Simply being responsive and giving one-time solutions doesn’t meet 7Geese standards. You value helping others become self-sufficient and enjoy creating structures and resources for learning.

HERE AT 7GEESE, SUPPORT MEANS YOU...

  • Are a rockstar prioritizer and can communicate priorities to the many people who will need you to help them.
  • Have a natural ability to adapt your tone and expressions to those who you’re helping.
  • Can express complex ideas simply and elegantly. Are not intimidated by technology or terminology.
  • Thrive off learning new things. You want to continuously progress in your role and take on more responsibility.
  • Have the ability to communicate across multiple time zones and cultures. (Don’t worry, you won’t have to work at 1am).
  • Are comfortable with debugging and quality testing. You won’t be coding, but you’re comfortable talking to developers and using products like Github to stay informed with what’s going on with product development.
  • Are excited to work with other 7Geese team members to implement process improvements, resources, and develop kick-ass support practices.

BREAKDOWN OF HOW YOUR TIME WILL BE SPENT:

  • 50% Reactive support via Intercom (incoming support questions, e-mails, phone calls, and potential visits). You know the product inside and out; other teams come to fact-check things with you like "Can a person with this permission do this?"
  • 25% Writing and maintaining content for our learning center and knowledge base. If there's nothing new to update, you're researching new best practices and creating new resources to add.
  • 10% Bug tracking and quality testing. As things get reported, you stay in the know and act as liaison between product development and the customer.
  • 10% Pro-active engagement (re-engagement emails, developing engagement campaigns to help champion our customers through ideal customer journeys).
  • 5% Reporting for monthly and quarterly meetings. Tracking KPIs, suggesting other metrics to track based on our goals, summarizing things like top 3 reasons for tickets in a given time period with suggested solutions.

YOUR EXPERIENCE!

  • You have a minimum of 1 year customer support experience, preferably in a SaaS / startup environment
  • You have demonstrated superhero like qualities in your current situation and are ready to soar into a new opportunity and take ownership of Customer Support at 7Geese
  • You have experience working in teams and are confident collaborating with others
  • Have exhibited our 3 core value areas: excellence, positivity, and growth
  • Are communication driven with experience explaining complex problems in easy ways
  • Have experience delivering solutions to others that help them move forward
  • Bonus: ability to do product screencasts, basic photo/video editing or graphic design to create content for the support center
