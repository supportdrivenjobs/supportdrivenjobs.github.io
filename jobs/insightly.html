Insightly Customer Service Specialist

Insightly is looking for a Customer Service Specialist in San Francisco who not only loves to help customers, but also genuinely cares about them. If you’re a team player who wants to help us continue to grow Insightly, we’d love to talk to you.

Insightly is dedicated to giving our customers fast, helpful, and friendly answers to any questions they have about our application. In this role, you’ll answer how-to questions, as well as questions about Insightly functionality and integrations. We have some of the most passionate customers worldwide, so sharing their feedback to influence where we take Insightly is a critical part of this role.

Reports to: Senior Customer Service Manager

Hours: Full time

When you apply (required), tell us briefly:

  • Why do you want to be part of the Insightly team?
  • Why are you the best candidate for this role?
  • What’s the best part of working in customer support?

Responsibilities

  • Become an Insightly product and features expert.
  • Help customers get the most out of our software and respond to customer requests via email ticket support, support requests that come up in our online community, and phone requests.
  • Escalate support requests and bugs to engineering team - triage, diagnose, and resolve technical issues and reduce support requests.
  • Develop and maintain relationships with QA and engineering teams for swift resolution of bugs, service interruptions, and other service-related issues.
  • Solve tickets using Zendesk, and help the team to get the most out of it.
  • Listen to customers’ needs effectively and share feedback with the engineering team.
  • Provide input to the Insightly Product team on feature improvements and customers’ business challenges.
  • Be a self-starter - identify and implement efficiencies and best practices. Identify trends/issues and come up with solutions.

Required Skills

  • Hands-on experience with cloud-based solutions
  • Experience with CRM a plus
  • Experience providing B2B a plus
  • Excellent written and communication skills
  • Ability to troubleshoot
  • Curiosity, and a passion for learning
  • Familiarity with Google Apps for Business
  • Basic computer skills including Microsoft Windows environment, Google Apps, and familiarity with various browsers
  • Must embody a great deal of patience
  • Be a customer service star with a technical background and troubleshooting skills
  • Familiarity with a ticket-based support tool (like Zendesk) and phone-based support
  • Must possess a great deal of customer empathy and have a good understanding of how to provide a great experience for customers
  • Comfortable with learning new technologies and solutions and answering customer concerns via phone, email and online communities
  • A passion for small business, CRM, and start-ups
  • Metrics-driven - can move quickly through tickets, yet maintain a high level of customer satisfaction

About this company

Over a million users in 150 countries rely on Insightly’s intuitive cloud-based CRM project management solution to track customer interactions and manage contacts, proposals, opportunities, projects and files every day. Insightly’s service integrates seamlessly with Gmail and Outlook, and it has consistently ranked as the No. 1 CRM application on the Google Apps Marketplace. Insightly recently concluded a Series C financing round and is backed by Emergence Capital Partners, Cloud Apps Management LC, Sozo ventures, and TrueBridge Capital Partners. We are based in San Francisco and conveniently located close to Muni, Bart, and Caltrain. We provide competitive compensation packages, stock options, full medical and other benefits.
