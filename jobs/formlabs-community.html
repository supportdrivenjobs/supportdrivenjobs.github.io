Formlabs Community Manager

Got questions about Formlabs?
@stephen can answer them in the Support Driven Chat

To reinvent an industry, you have to build the best team. Join Formlabs if you want to bring ground-breaking professional 3D printers to the desktop of every designer, engineer, researcher, and artist in the world.

Our primary goal is to give our customers the best tools with an amazing experience. With users of all levels throughout the world, developing our presence online is essential to growing our customers and product. We’re looking for a Community Manager to be the voice of Formlabs users, at events and online.

If you are ready to take 3D printing users to the next level by celebrating their applications, and teaching them how to do even more, join the Form Team as our Community Manager.

The job:

Inspire, organize, and scale our community presence through a variety of media
Cultivate powerful connections between users to share best practices and projects
Get the world excited about 3D printing

You:

Are already an expert in CAD and evangelist for 3D printing
Maintain an active presence thorough forums, social media, and events
Excited to be the voice and face of Formlabs in online webinars and local meetups
A creative and patient problem solver who loves to help others be successful
Thoughtful and responsive, with a natural curiosity and sense of humor
Can quickly develop quality written and visual content to teach tips and concepts
Translate feedback from the community into insights for core internal teams
Can crunch numbers to track feedback and build metrics
An independent and driven self-starter, comfortable in a start-up environment
Possess exceptional written and verbal communication skills

Bonus skills:

Hands-on fabrication expertise
Previous experience in the 3D printing industry
Personal interest in design or entrepreneurship
