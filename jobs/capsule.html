Capsule Customer Support

Got questions about Capsule?

Since 2009 we’ve been making it easy for business to take care of their customers and sales opportunities. Capsule is now one of the most popular CRM’s for small business and sales teams.

We’re expanding our support team and looking for someone who loves helping people out. Troubleshooting and communication will be a big part of your job and you’ll be comfortable using email & twitter to keep our customers happy. You’ll also spend some time working with our development and design teams to share customer feedback and test new features.

We’re a UK based company with an office in central Manchester. We’re open to remote working if you’re outside of Manchester, but you need to be in the UK for this position. You’ll be joining a small, dedicated & friendly team that cares deeply about the customer experience. We offer a start-up style culture with a competitive salary and benefits.

What we’re looking for:

  • You have experience helping customers through a previous marketing or support-related position
  • You’re tech savvy with an interest in web and mobile apps
  • You’ll be a great writer with a positive style and enjoy describing solutions simply
  • You’re able to read between the lines when necessary and minimise emails back-and-forth to get to a solution quickly
  • You’re self motivated and confident working independently but you also know your limits and when to seek direction
  • You’re a pro at minimising disruptions whether working remotely or in the office and can get through a high volume of emails each day

A typical week involves:

  • Helping people get the most out of Capsule by responding to emails and tweets
  • Troubleshooting and detailing issues for our developers to resolve
  • Sharing customer feedback with the design and development team
  • Creating and updating helpful online support guides
  • You’ll also spend some time contributing to scaling and improving our internal support practices

We offer very competitive salaries and benefits

  • Flexible working hours
  • Option to work from home one day a week
  • 25 days paid annual leave in addition to the stat holidays
  • Private medical, dental and optical insurance
  • Pension scheme with generous matched contributions
  • Casual dress environment
  • The best kit for the job (which usually means lots of Apple gear)
  • Monthly eBook allowance with your choice of iPad Mini or Kindle
  • Mobile phone (e.g. Android or iPhone)
  • Conference allowance

How to apply:

If this excites you and your experience and strengths match what we’re looking for send us an email introducing yourself.

Working at Capsule

Do what you love
