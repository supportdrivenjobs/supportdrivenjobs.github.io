Code Climate Customer Success Manager

Code Climate is the world's largest static analysis platform, helping over 50,000 developers improve the health of their codebases. We work hard to provide clear, timely and actionable insights derived from source code. We recently launched the Code Climate Platform, the first open, extensible platform for all types of static analysis.

Code Climate is available on our hosted platform for free for open source projects, and offers paid subscriptions to analyze private source code either on our hosted platform or Code Climate Enterprise, our behind the firewall product. Our customers include Fortune 500s, public and private companies, large and small, with a range of technical sophistication and exposure to static analysis.

As a Customer Success Manager you’ll work with customers to help them achieve their software goals, primarily (but not necessarily exclusively) using Code Climate. You’ll also work closely with our engineering and customer service teams as a voice for these customers within the company.

As a key part of the external face of Code Climate, we're looking for someone who is invested in the success of our customers, and represents our values well by putting customers first and executing rigorously.

Examples of problems you'll be solving:

  • Becoming an active and positive collaborator in the customer’s mission to better improve their code and software development processes by learning about their unique goals, successes and challenges.
  • Navigating organizations to determine who are the influencers and decision makers.
  • Training clients and promoting wider and deeper Code Climate adoption within their organizations.
  • Educating customers with unique software development processes and environments about the best way to use Code Climate.
  • Concierge onboarding for customers with large teams of developers to ensure smooth integration of Code Climate into their organization
  • Integrating Code Climate into an organization’s existing IT environment -- including source code management, code review tools, and collaboration tools like Trello and Slack.
  • Diagnosing issues affecting customers and collaborating with the product engineering team to identify solutions (and creatively suggesting workarounds, when necessary).

You're awesome because:

  • You elicit glowing testimonials from customers.
  • You communicate clearly, concisely and honestly. Customers trust you.
  • You empathize with issues customers face and are compelled to solve them.
  • You continually advocate internally on behalf of customers, and can name from memory the problems that our biggest customers face.
  • You're never afraid to strike up a conversation with someone new, online or in person.
  • You’re comfortable having discussions with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
  • You're able to navigate complex organizations.
  • You're technologically curious and always learning.

Working at Code Climate

  • Join a small team of around fifteen full-time people who love what they do.
  • Competitive compensation and meaningful stock options.
  • Flexible and generous paid time off including an open vacation policy and maternity/paternity leave.
  • Health, dental and vision insurance. We pay 100% of the premium and 50% of the premium for dependents.
  • Company retreats twice a year. So far we've gone to Bali and San Diego.
  • Opportunities to continue your professional development, including attending tech conferences.
  • Dog-friendly office stocked with snacks, drinks and cold-brew iced coffee.
