Illuminate Education Support Specialist

Got questions about Illuminate Education?
Matt Dale (@md) or anyone else from Illuminate Education can answer them in the Support Driven Chat

We're looking for a few friendly Support Specialists to join our team. You'll have the opportunity to support a great product, make a difference in the education community, and work with some really amazing people.

A typical day involves:

  • Helping teachers and educators use Illuminate's products to make a difference in student's lives.
  • Handling problems using the software, providing timely workarounds for our clients, and documenting issues for the development team so we can continually make our products better.
  • Being the friendly "voice of Illuminate" to our clients on the phone.
  • Providing clear, timely, helpful responses to email requests.
  • Collaborating with Illuminators on other teams (implementation, data, development, etc.) to make sure our clients get the best support possible.
  • Continually learning and growing so you can be a better support specialist, and a better human being!

Being a support specialist requires:

  • Amazing people skills
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Motivated, self-directed learner
  • Strong problem-solving/troubleshooting skills
  • Strong technical aptitude
  • Must be a quick learner
  • Good sense of humor
  • Efficient office work habits
  • Ability to work independently and in a team

Meet Illuminate:

Illuminate Education is a rapidly growing EdTech, SaaS provider founded by educators, for educators. We offer next-gen web-based software tools for the K12 market. Our streamlined suite of products is a one-stop solution for comprehensive data management and analysis, enabling users at every level of the educational system – county, district, site, classroom, student and parent – efficient access to the information essential to their success.
