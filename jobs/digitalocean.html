Digital Ocean Trust & Safety Manager

Reporting to the Director of Customer Support, the Trust & Safety Manager at DigitalOcean will lead a team of 11 specialists who ensure our customers have a positive experience on our platform while reducing the impact of bad actions. To excel in this role you should be passionate about people, process and product as you will be guiding all three. This is a great opportunity for an experienced, performance-oriented manager looking for a great new leadership opportunity. You’ll have the chance to portray thought leadership for what Trust & Safety as a practice is at DigitalOcean.

You will owns all aspects of creating and maintaining processes to continue developing a premier team that effectively handles abuse, law enforcement requests and customer verification. You should also bring a high level of domain experience to the organization. These skills include customer communications, creating internal policies/external policies, and ultimately helping to keep our network safe and abuse free. Finally, this person should also possess excellent interpersonal, communication and management skills. As a leader in our organization, you will have the ability to influence both product and policy decisions with internal teams. This is a full-time position based in our New York City office.

What You'll Be Doing:

  • Performs 1-on-1 meetings with direct reports as needed and on a regular basis to help motivate and manage their performance
  • Create process guidelines to handle customer verification, inbound abuse and network abuse, law enforcement requests, and outside information sharing
  • Lead a 24/7 team to ensure compliance with processes (you don’t have to work 24/7 though!)
  • Develop relationships with product, security and legal to enhance team productivity and performance
  • Introduces new methods to our processes and oversees their implementation and effectiveness
  • Collaborate with shift managers to ensure continuity in building a strong management team
  • Establish a recruiting plan to attract, hire, onboard, develop, excite and manage high performing team members
  • Develop, measure, and develop team KPIs such as: rate of abuse, RBL listings, FBL volume, etc.
  • Own the communication with the product team to improve the team’s tools
  • Reduces overall costs of handling abuse
  • Own relationships with key outside parties such as law enforcement, ensuring overall compliance with legal procedures

What We'll Expect From You:

  • Exhibits and lives the servant leadership style and has prior experience as a manager.
  • Understands fraud and abuse within a cloud infrastructure environment and has experience effectively combating it.
  • Creates a climate in which people want to do their best; can motivate many kinds of direct reports and team or project members, empowering others.
  • Is dedicated to meeting the expectations required of internal and external customers.
  • Gets first hand customer information and uses it for improvements in service.
  • Establishes and maintains effective relationships with customer and internal team members, gaining their trust and respect.
  • Spends his/her time and the time of others on what’s essential to success.
  • Can quickly sense what will help or hinder accomplishing a goal, eliminating roadblocks and creating focus.
  • Has the ability to get their hands dirty with customer facing tasks while displaying the capacity to delegate as needed.
  • Is personally committed and actively works to improve self through on the job and outside learning.
  • Is able to write clearly and effectively in reviewing direct reports, responding to customer inquires and follow-ups.
  • Expresses the capacity to manage a distributed team, across multiple timezones and shifts.
  • Displays empathy to both internal and external customer requests.
  • Showcases the ability to encourage and facilitate mentoring and knowledge sharing within the Support team
  • Strongly identify with our brand and team culture
  • Prior experience in similar environments

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field and/or at least 4-5+ years of related experience
  • Technical expertise with Linux and other OpenSource technologies is ESSENTIAL; this person will be an integral and hands-on member of the team at large
  • Experience as a technical or support lead/supervisor/manager, ideally in a similar IaaS, service provider or cloud computing environment
  • Direct experience with managing, coaching, and mentoring team members
  • Exceptional verbal and written communications skills
  • Strong analytical skills and pattern recognition
  • Exceptional attention to detail and follow-up
  • Experience with staffing, interviewing and team building
