Skillcrush Customer Support Specialist, part-time

Skillcrush is looking for a Customer Support Specialist to join our Operations team! This is a part-time, entry-level role. The ideal candidate will work 20 hours a week on an adjusted schedule - roughly 5 hours per day, Friday through Sunday.

As a Customer Support Specialist, your main responsibility will be to make sure our potential and current students have answers to their questions and are all set for their Skillcrush courses.

You’ll be communicating with students via email and sometimes live chat so you should look forward to crafting messages most of the day. You should be able to pass on detailed information and instructions quickly and clearly while keeping the fun, supportive, and friendly attitude students have come to expect from Skillcrush.

The cases you’ll be handling will range from routine FAQs to very unique situations so you should be happy to be doing both repetitive work and solving problems in creative ways. You should be comfortable making decisions on your own but also be ready to regularly “pair” with other team members when the situation calls for it.

You’re a good fit for this role if you’re a genuinely positive and supportive person who’s also meticulous, organized, and dependable to a fault.

Since our mission here at Skillcrush is to help our students get the tech skills they need to have careers they love, you should be interested in (if not passionate about!) tech and genuinely excited to help our students succeed.

Responsibilities:

  • Answer potential and current students’ questions and resolve problems via email and live chat.
  • Investigate and resolve issues with payments and/or access.
  • Document customer support processes and policies to make sure no one team member is the only one who knows how to do something.
  • Share student insights with other teams, as appropriate.
  • Moderate student comments during live webinars, and support the Marketing team with other sales initiatives, as needed.

Required:

  • Experience directly supporting customers
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Fun and friendly attitude with a true passion for helping and supporting people
  • Proactive and can-do approach to challenges
  • A love of both routines and surprises (Every day will be filled with both!)
  • Sense of urgency and excitement to find answers and solve problems
  • An enjoyment of working independently and as part of a team
  • Honesty, outstanding attention to detail, and great organizational skills
  • The ability to take your work seriously but have a good time doing it!
  • Access to a reliable, high-speed Internet connection
  • Available (and excited!) to work Friday through Monday, preferably between 9am-5pm in the U.S. Eastern time zone

Bonuses:

  • Familiarity with any of the web design or development topics we teach, e.g. WordPress, HTML and CSS, or visual design
  • Experience working remotely with team members in different time zones
  • Experience with Help Scout, Olark, WordPress, JIRA, or similar tools
  • Experience working on an Agile/Scrum team
  • Fondness for sharing gifs, emoji, or other general online happiness with your teammates ;)

Salary Range:

$25-35/hour, commensurate with skills and experience

Perks:

  • Flexible, remote work
  • Competitive benefits package
  • Coworking space credits
  • Free access to all the Skillcrush classes you can eat
  • A fun, passionate team working from all over the world
  • A fast growing company with lots of opportunities for job growth

Culture

Skillcrush is a rapidly growing tech education company that offers online classes, workshops, and blueprints on a variety of technology topics.

At Skillcrush, we have built an open and collaborative environment. We hire the people we want to work with but who also think differently than us and challenge the status quo. We empower team members by giving them the support and autonomy they need to succeed. We embrace data to make decisions and overcome obstacles. We do what we do well, but we’re always looking to do it better.

Skillcrush is an equal opportunity employer and evaluates applicants regardless of an individual’s age, race, color, gender, religion, national origin (or current homebase), sexual orientation, disability, veteran status, family status, timezone, geography, musical preferences or college football allegiances. We think a diverse team builds a better product!
