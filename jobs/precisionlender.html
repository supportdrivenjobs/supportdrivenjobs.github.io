PrecisionLender Client Support Superhero

As a Client Support Superhero, you will support, educate, and empower our clients and end-users so that they can be successful. You will own the client support experience and work to exceed clients' expectations by genuinely caring about them, personally & professionally.

What Your Superpowers Are:

  • Passionately helping others.
  • Explaining complex issues in beautifully simple terms.
  • Listening way more than you talk and connecting with people.
  • Embracing change & adapting quickly.
  • Managing multiple projects simultaneously.
  • A natural ability to see the full scope of a problem, in order to ask the right questions.
  • Never letting a detail fall through the cracks.
  • Using social media or web-based software, including Twitter, Facebook, Google Apps.
  • Familiarity with common help-desk/ticketing solutions such as Zendesk and Jira.

How You Will Use Your Powers:

  • Saving clients across the country from any issues they are having.
  • Evaluate, troubleshoot, and follow-up on client issues as well as replicate and document for further escalation.
  • Develop support content including articles, blog posts, videos and other collateral. You may not author all that content, but you will work with our development and consulting teams to identify content needs and get them filled.
  • Being a voice for clients and fighting for their causes.
  • Drive product change and improvement by working with our Product Development team to document changes and improvements to our software and ensure that our Clients are aware of those changes.
  • Foster our community of users to stimulate the sharing of ideas so that we can get our best clients talking to each other and cross-pollinating those ideas.

Your Experience & Education:

You may be fresh out of college or you may have 10 years under your belt - either way, you need to have spent significant time working in a business setting, ideally in areas like customer service, internal and external client support.
Bachelors Degree preferred

How You Should Apply:

We're looking for people who pay attention and care about their future. If you want to be considered for this position, you have to answer the application questions, this is us testing your attention to detail.

The final step in our hiring process will be for you to arrange personal reference calls with former supervisors as well as others we may choose. If you're awesome, that part will be easy.
