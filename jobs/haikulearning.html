What is Haiku Learning?

Haiku Learning is about community, and that includes our company’s community of creators, evangelists and visionaries. Right now we’re looking for a talented communicator and process-driven people-person who is passionate about great software, smart teamwork, and doing good in the world. We are a technology company that is passionate about the technology, but even more passionate about making tools that transform learning.

Why Haiku Learning is one of the best places to work on the planet:

  • You get to promote learning spaces that change lives.
  • You get to work with teachers and education technologists - some of the smartest, most amazing people from the leading schools and educational programs in the world.

If you like people, are passionate about education, and want to help build the learning spaces of tomorrow, we want to hear from you.

Role Summary

Support Specialist are in charge of making sure every support request is met with positive energy and attention. Support Specialists should have a good eye for detail and are excellent writers who want to make sure people are taken care of. They are sleuths and investigators: Think Sherlock (with social skills).

As a First-Line-of-Defense Support Specialists, your primary role is to be responsive to customers via phone and our support ticketing system. You will also be investigating problems customers encounter to see if you can identify bugs and other issues. Support Specialists follow up internally as well as externally to communicate findings and solutions.

The expectation of the job is that you will be working 40 hours per week. You will be part of our support rotation on some weekends and holidays, and your weekly hours will be flexed to compensate for these duties. Your exact schedule will be determined by you and your supervisor.

Support Desk

  • Provide general support, lightweight integrations support, and general troubleshooting
  • Respond to support tickets, phone calls, and emails
  • Provide weekend Helpdesk support on a rotating basis
  • Follow up on existing tickets in a timely manner
  • Maintain active dialogue with users on open tickets
  • Report bugs and propose fixes to the Technical Lead or Technical Services Team
  • Understand and follow internal processes for support emergencies
  • Give suggestions for internal support processes and tool improvements

Product Quality Assurance & Technical Investigation

  • Work with Technical Lead to provide higher-level support for complex issues, including deep investigation, bug replication, and troubleshooting as needed
  • Participate in pre-launch Happy Hours (Quality Assurance Testing)
  • Participate in post-launch QA and customer follow-up process
  • Make tickets for fixes, changes, and enhancements
  • Work with Support Technical Lead to prioritize issues and follow up on critical bugs

Other Responsibilities

  • Attend weekly departmental meetings
  • Be responsive to our internal teams via chat, video, and email during working hours
  • Help keep Knowledgebase articles and videos accurate, up to date, and cross-referenced
  • Collaborate with Community Care team on general and shared duties
  • Collaborate with Support Team to triage tickets and escalate problems as needed
  • Complete other tasks and projects as requested by Team Manager

Skills & Proficiences

  • Strong technical skills with the ability to troubleshoot and clearly communicate solutions both orally and in writing
  • Familiarity with Google Apps and ticket tracking applications not required, but is a plus

Requirements

  • Bachelors degree, or comparable experience in customer support
