Captain401 Account Specialist

Captain401 is hiring our first Account Specialist, who will be responsible for ensuring that our customers have an exceptional experience with Captain401. Working closely with our Account Management team, the Account Specialist will build relationships with our customers by handling customer support inquiries (via email and phone) and troubleshooting issues related to our customers' 401(k) retirement accounts.

Our customers are at the core of our business, and the Account Specialist's role is crucial in giving them the best experience possible. You'll be on the front lines helping people make the most of their 401(k) savings and get on the right path to a secure financial future.

This role is at the intersection of operations, sales, and engineering, so the Account Specialist must work well with different parts of the company.

There is no typical day in a startup, so you will have to be adaptable — there will be no shortage of interesting challenges to tackle!

Requirements

  • Exceptional phone-based and email-based communication skills
  • Ability to empathize with customers and a passion for delivering a great customer experience
  • Excellent organizational skills and task management abilities
  • Attention to detail —- no issue slips through the cracks
  • Minimum 1 year of work experience (experience working in customer-facing role at a startup is a plus)
  • Passion for solving big problems and having an immediate and positive impact
  • Industry or domain experience not required

Our Benefits

As an employee benefits company, benefits are very important to us, and Captain401's philosophy is to be generous when it comes to employees' wellness. Here's what we currently offer:

  • A great 401(k) plan: our own! Our 401(k) includes a dollar-for-dollar employer match up to 4% of compensation.
  • Top-of-the-line health insurance plans, as well as dental and vision insurance, with premiums fully paid for by the company.
  • An annual membership to One Medical Group, with membership fees paid by Captain401.
  • Commuter benefits.
  • FSA with an employer contribution of $250 per year.
  • Dollar-for-dollar match of employee donations to non-profit 501(c)(3) organizations, up to $500 per year.
  • Healthy snacks and drinks available at our office.
  • Team retreats and outings.

We try not to spend money on “perks” like ping pong tables. We practice what we preach! We believe employer funds are best spent towards investing in the long-term physical, financial, and emotional well-being of our employees.
