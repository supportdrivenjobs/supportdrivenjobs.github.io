Redgate Software Customer Support Manager

Redgate is looking for a Customer Support Manager to lead our Customer Support team. Our ideal candidate is someone who’s looking for a challenge – not just leading our excellent Customer Support team and taking customer support at Redgate to the next level, but also thinking about support in commercial terms and helping us to realise the full value of our world-class support offering.

Reporting to the Head of Sales & Customer Support, you’ll collaborate with Redgate’s Commercial Team to build and execute the strategy for Customer Support. You’ll be leading team leaders who manage the rest of the team across our Cambridge and Pasadena offices. You’ll be responsible for setting direction and monitoring the performance of the team, as well as for the continuous improvement of the team.

With a deep passion for customer service, you’ll be aware of current best practice and will network with like-minded people. You’ll be constantly looking for areas of improvement and will look outside of our business for innovation and ideas.

Confident with data, you’ll be able to analyse and use the data the team collects to inform other areas of the business. You’ll be comfortable collaborating with product managers and sales managers to drive innovation and improvements across the business.

Redgate views customer support as a competitive advantage, and therefore a crucial part of the business. This is an exciting time to be a part of our ever-improving team.

Redgate is proud to be independently recognised as Cambridge’s best workplace as well as an award winning software provider – we aspire to be the best place you’ve ever worked. To apply, simply upload your CV and covering letter or get in touch if you’d like to talk to us first.

What makes you Redgate’s next Customer Support Manager?

  • You’ve got a genuine passion for customer experience and the ability to work across an organisation to make continuous improvements in this area.
  • You can demonstrate experience which makes your customer service knowledge and team management skills obvious to us.
  • You’ll be able to translate and report on the large amount of data available.
  • You have experience of taking an idea, building a plan and executing projects which involve collaboration with other teams. You have a genuine interest outside of your area and work for the greater good.
  • You can demonstrate an ability to think commercially and are comfortable working with revenue targets.
  • You have experience of striking the right balance between operational demands and project work
  • You have a genuine interest in technology and are able to learn our technical products and tools
  • You take management seriously, and will propagate the Redgate management way.
  • You’re a team builder. You’re able to learn and adapt to Redgate’s culture and have the skills to build and grow your team in this way.
  • You value integrity and are an authentic, courageous and confident manager – you understand how to empower your team to do great work.
  • You can operate successfully in a rapidly changing, fast moving environment, whilst providing direction for your team.
  • You have an action bias. When faced by uncertainty and risk, you make decisions nonetheless, learning from mistakes.
  • You’ve got excellent communication and collaboration skills, which are effective across departments and countries.
  • You foster innovation and creativity and have experience managing change.
  • You’re comfortable with transparency and feedback.

What would help?

  • History of managing customer support in a software business, understanding the complexities of supporting a large amount of desktop tools across different platforms.
  • Demonstrable commercial experience in a customer support context.

What’s the package?

  • A great benefits package.
  • Salary is £40 – £60k per annum, depending on experience.
  • You’ll work about 40 hours a week, with flexible work time.
