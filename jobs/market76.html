Market76 Client Success Specialist

As a Client Success Specialist, you'll be the face and voice of the company for our clients. You're a natural people-person and are energized when you can make the difference between customers being disheartened or delighted. You're an excellent communicator and have a knack for organization and attention to detail.
At the end of the day, you know what it takes to make customers happy and keep the trains running on time.
This role is critical to ensuring we provide an excellent service to all of our clients and will be relied on by every other team, be that as a liaison for Engineering or backup for Sales.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Provide excellent customer service to customers through phone, email, and chat.
  • Field questions from users, troubleshoot their issues, and provide them with concise solutions.
  • Effectively communicate with the Engineering team to fix issues that arise and suggest improvements to our overall customer experience.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to think on your feet and quickly troubleshoot problems
  • Ability to communicate potentially complicated issues to people in terms they'll understand.
  • Strong proficiency with Microsoft Office / Google Apps.
  • Great organizational skills and attention to detail
  • (Bonus) Proficiency with CRM/service systems like Salesforce, Jira, Zendesk, etc.)
  • (Bonus) Experience writing SQL queries

BENEFITS

  • Competitive pay
  • Equity in the business
  • Office snacks and team outings
  • Medical, Dental, Life, Disability Insurance

Culture

Market76 provides a place to discover, purchase, and implement the best products, services, and software to achieve every investor's financial goals.

In under 140 words:
People have a right to choose how to protect and grow their wealth.

This right requires having good options; it requires easily accessible and clear information about those options; and it requires the frictionless ability to exercise that choice.

Market76 is building the world's most investor-centric wealth management marketplace; a place to discover, purchase, and implement the best products, services, and software for each investor.

Market76 unites financial advisors and investors and provides them the benefit of shared scale. We negotiate the best pricing and product access for everyone in the marketplace, and provide technology to connect all of the pieces.

Our platform provides scale and distribution efficiency to sellers. It informs buyers by gathering and communicating the information necessary to make the smartest choices. It makes implementation easy by helping all members use their information in our applications and others.
