Ray Customer Support Specialist

The Ray Super Remote hit the market in October with the world’s first touchscreen universal remote control. We’re a small team taking on an entire industry with the simplest, most user-friendly device to control the modern home.

We are in the exciting phase of building our entire customer support experience. We are looking for a Customer Support Specialist to join us at the frontline and be a part of envisioning, shaping and evolving this crucial part of Ray.

This is a full-time 40hr/week position based in Dumbo, Brooklyn.

YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Be on the front-line building relationships with customers over email, chat and phone.
  • Get to know Ray and all the devices we support, inside-out and upside down. You’ll need to be comfortable troubleshooting both common and rare situations, with tech savvy and not-so-savvy customers alike.
  • Genuinely care about our customers. Their problems are your problems. Find ways to delight meaningfully and redefine what it means to go the extra mile.
  • Reliably resolve a customer’s questions and needs efficiently, completely and accurately. Own it, but don’t be afraid to ask for help.
  • Be a team player and take initiative. Share your learning, build our knowledge base and improve the way we work.

ABOUT YOU:

  • You are a great communicator with excellent verbal and written English skills: You are concise, clear and focused in your responses while expressing personality in a fun, friendly and professional tone.
  • You are self-driven and motivated to learn about technology: We don’t expect you to arrive talking in IR code sets, but you are expected to dive in and ramp up pretty quickly.
  • You are a resourceful problem solver: This is complicated stuff, you’ll need to dissect a customer’s situation and find nifty solutions.
  • You are upbeat, positive, patient and sensitive to the customer: Their mood, how much handholding they need and the appropriate tone you should take to ease their minds.
  • You love TV, entertainment and the connected home: we are changing the world’s relationship with TV and their homes, you need to love it as much as our customers do.
  • You have previous experience in a customer support role (2 + years). Being proficient with Zendesk is a major plus.
  • Bonus points if you have experience in a startup, consumer electronics and/or interacting with customers on Amazon.

BENEFITS:

You pay us for this once in a lifetime opportunity. J/k.

  • Medical, dental and vision insurance
  • Employee stock options
  • Unlimited vacation
  • Company outings & gatherings
  • Snacks & drinks on tap

ABOUT RAY:

Ray controls thousands of home devices over infrared and wifi technology, including TVs, set-top boxes, streaming boxes, audio devices and more. It’s hooked up to your TV provider to recommend what to watch on TV based on what you like. It’s designed for everyone and heralded as having the simplest, quickest setup known to humankind.
